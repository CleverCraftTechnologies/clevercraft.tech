Who we are

CleverCraft Capital (CleverCraft Technologies Inc.) is a proprietary trading company based in Canada. We focus on liquid, exchange-traded futures and operate with a disciplined, technology-first approach. Our activities are internal: we do not solicit or manage outside capital and we do not offer advisory or brokerage services.

Research

Systematic and discretionary frameworks informed by market structure, volatility regimes, and session behavior.

Execution

Exchange-traded instruments only (e.g., index futures) with pre-trade checklists and strict risk limits.

Tooling

Internal dashboards for journaling, P&L attribution, and rule compliance; automation for routine workflows.

Operating model

We participate in funded trading programs with established third-party firms or deploy internal capital. Revenue arises from trading performance in regulated markets. No public products are sold and no client accounts are accepted.

  • Evaluation → allocation via reputable prop programs.
  • Risk-first gameplay: fixed daily loss, max drawdown, position limits.
  • Post-trade analytics and weekly playbook updates.
  • Zero involvement with cryptocurrencies or virtual assets.
