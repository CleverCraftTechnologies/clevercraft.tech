Who we are

CleverCraft Technologies Inc. is a private, technology-driven proprietary trading and analytics company based in Canada. We focus on liquid, exchange-traded futures and operate with a disciplined, software-first approach. Our activities are entirely internal: we do not solicit or manage outside capital and we do not offer advisory, brokerage, or investment management services.

Research

Systematic and discretionary frameworks informed by market structure, volatility regimes, and session behavior.

Execution

Exchange-traded instruments only (e.g., index futures) with pre-trade checklists and strict risk limits.

Tooling

Internal dashboards for journaling, P&L attribution, rule compliance, and automation for routine workflows.

Operating model

We participate in funded trading programs with established third-party proprietary trading firms and deploy internal capital where appropriate. Revenue arises from trading performance in regulated futures markets. No public products are sold and no client accounts are accepted; all trading is conducted for the company’s own benefit.

  • Evaluation → allocation through reputable prop-firm programs.
  • Risk-first approach: fixed daily loss, max drawdown, and position limits.
  • Post-trade analytics and weekly playbook updates.
  • Zero involvement with cryptocurrencies or virtual assets.
